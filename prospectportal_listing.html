Ask Us About Affordable Units


The purpose of the Low and Moderate program is to provide affordable housing to low-income households by limiting the maximum rent that can be charged for a unit and by requiring that some or all of the units be made available only to households with incomes below a percentage (e.g. 80% & 120%) of the Average Median Income. Prospects must qualify, 12+ month lease terms only.
